WHOLESALE DISTRIBUTION

Providing World Class Service Experiences!..

Falcon Global General Trading initiated wholesale distribution division to cater the needs of wholesale clients like hypermarkets, supermarkets, grocery stores, cafeterias and ship chandlers. Today we are equipped with advanced mobile technology and logistics options with trained staff to initiate a smooth buying experience. Our stores are strategically located across UAE, where our customers can easily visit and purchase goods at reasonable prices. Our van sales division also helps our customers to get their goods on time. Our expertise gained from working for years in the industry have enabled us to understand market trends in Middle East and this helps us to make available the right products at the right time that are required by our clients.
